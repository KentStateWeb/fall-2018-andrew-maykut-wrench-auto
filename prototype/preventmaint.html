PREVENTATIVE MAINTENANCE

Though taking time out from your schedule to do preventative maintenance may seem like just one more “to do” to add to your already busy schedule, caring for your car can save you loads of money in the future by actually preventing issues with your car down the line.

Wrench Automotive is available to assist you with a variety of preventative maintenance services. Our expert auto mechanics can help you to make sure that your car is safe and functioning optimally, keeping you on the road. Here are just a few of the services that we offer:

We can check your brakes. Squeaking, squealing, grinding. These are all symptoms of a brake system problem or worn out brake pads or shoes . If these are noises you hear regularly, stop in so we can check your brakes.

We can evaluate your transmission. A transmission can be expensive to repair. Prevent these costly repairs with a transmission fluid check and fluid replacement / or a transmission service as needed.

We can change your oil. Oil changes remove contaminants, sludge and dirt that builds up as your engine works. Dirty oil – or worse, no oil – can permanently damage your engine. We can quickly change your oil to keep your engine running smoothly.

We can check your coolant levels. The role of coolant is to keep your engine running at an optimal temperature. Coolant is needed in all seasons, even the coldest weather. Rad Air’s auto specialists can quickly check your coolant and get you back on the road.

We can get you new tires. Buying tires shouldn’t be a hassle. Our tire experts can let you know if you need new tires and recommend the best fit for your vehicle, driving style and budget.

Let your car perform as it was designed to by doing some preventative maintenance on a schedule.

  • This will allow you to do repairs when and where you choose. It saves you time, money, and eliminates almost all breakdowns in your vehicle.
  • Don’t let your car keep you from where you’re going. A well maintained car provides better gas mileage, prolongs the life of your vehicle, and keeps you safe on the road.
  • Preventative Maintenance can save you from the large expensive repairs down the road.

Wrench Automotive can help you to prevent some of the most costly repairs that can happen to automobiles. Let us help you to keep your repair costs down with our comprehensive preventative maintenance. Stop in or call today for a convenient appointment!
